About the group

Mandarin PlayGroup (San Francisco) started with a simple idea: our little ones learn Mandarin best when they're having fun with friends. So we meet at playgrounds around the city — a different neighborhood each time — and let the kids do what they do best: play.

You don't need to be fluent. Plenty of us are learning right alongside our toddlers, and every level of Mandarin makes the group better.
